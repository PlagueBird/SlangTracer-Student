chuck adj.

[Chuck n.]

(US black) white.

1978J. Webb Fields of Fire (1980) 212: You better not go, Baggder. He don\xe2\x80\x99t want to see any Chuck dudes.
1990(con. c.1970) G. Hasford Phantom Blooper 188: A dustoff dee-dee\xe2\x80\x99d friendly Whiskey India Alphas to Charlie Med, most ricky-tick. Them chuck squid pecker-checkers were number one.

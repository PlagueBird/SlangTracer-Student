plant v.2

1. (Irish) to hit.

1842Flash (N.Y.) 10 July 2/2: Lilly got a pretty hard one on the pimple, dodged another and [...] planted a severe facer.
1853N.Y. Clipper n.p.: Jones made several attempts to plant upon him .
1866Wild Boys of London I 192/1: He planted one fair on the nose of his opponent.
1878\xe2\x80\x939A. Garcia Tough Trip Through Paradise (1977) 37: I have a good mind to plant you right here, you two-faced sneak.
1889H. O\xe2\x80\x99Reilly Fifty Years on the Trail 169: I planted him one in the \xe2\x80\x98bread basket\xe2\x80\x99.
1894Mirror of Life 14 Apr. 2/2: Imagine [...] Peter Jackson portraying the part of the old decrepit nigger, and instead of pleading [...] for mercy, walking up to the slave-driver and planting his \xe2\x80\x98auctioneer\xe2\x80\x99 on the jaw!
1914Sport (Adelaide) 2 Oct. 5/4: Mind the pommy does not plant you one, old boy.
1918A.N. Depew Gunner Depew 16: I planted him one in the kidneys and another in the mouth.
1934Joe Archibald \xe2\x80\x98Time Will Tell\xe2\x80\x99 in Phantom Detective Sept. \xe2\x80\x98Shut up, ya old devil!\xe2\x80\x99 he snarled huskily. \xe2\x80\x98Or I\xe2\x80\x99ll plant ya.\xe2\x80\x99.
1985N. Pileggi Wiseguy (2001) 20: Jimmy could plant you just as fast as shake your hand.
1991(con. 1920s) O.D. Brooks Legs 159: I planted him there with the ball of my hand. It spattered blood all over his kisser.
2000F. Mac Anna Cartoon City 205: He stuck out his chin. \xe2\x80\x98Here look, plant one on me, I mean it. If anyone deserves a fist sandwich, then it\xe2\x80\x99s me.\xe2\x80\x99.

2. (Irish) to kill, to shoot dead.

1890A.C. Gunter Miss Nobody of Nowhere 27: Shoot your man dead in this community fust, and call him a liar afterward; otherwise, you\xe2\x80\x99ll be planted before you\xe2\x80\x99ve got used to the climate.
1900A.H. Lewis \xe2\x80\x98Jess\xe2\x80\x99 in Sandburrs 22: This yere Watkins is goin\xe2\x80\x99 to die. You\xe2\x80\x99ve planted him.

3. of a man, to have sexual intercourse.

2001K. Sampson Outlaws (ms.) 76: The lad starts to straightforward fuck her, him on top planting her dead hard and dead fast.

canary n.2

[\xe2\x80\x98in true descent from the cod-piece, though not so glaring in its declaration\xe2\x80\x99 (Ware)]

an ornament worn at the hip.

1857 advert in \xe2\x80\x98Ducange Anglicus\xe2\x80\x99 Vulgar Tongue (1857) 45: A pair of Kerseymere Kicksies, any colour, built very slap-up, with the artful dodge, a canary, very hanky-panky, with a double fakement.
